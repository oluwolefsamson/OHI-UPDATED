Our story
Olympian House International (OHI) was foun-
ded in 2015 by ﬁlmmaker and development 
communication specialist Fombang Banns N. 
driven by a simple but powerful  belief:  stories  
have  the  ability  to  move people and when 
people move, change follows. 
Born  out  of  a  lifelong  passion  for  capturing  
human experience, OHI exists to make Africa’s 
development and investment journeys visible, 
relatable and unforgettable. We understand 
that behind every project, every policy and 
every investment, there are people whose lives 
reﬂect the true meaning of impact.
Over the years, OHI has grown into a trusted 
partner for  NGOs, DFIs,  investors  and  govern-
ments  across the continent. Our work blends 
creativity with strategy  to  translate  complex 
initiatives  into  authentic, human-centered  
visual  stories  that  inspire  conﬁdence, spark 
collaboration and unlock opportunities.
Together, let’s tell the stories that build the 
Africa we imagine.
